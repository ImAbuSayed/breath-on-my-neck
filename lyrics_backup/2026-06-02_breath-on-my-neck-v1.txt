[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Dust dancing in the fading light
I want to lose track of time tonight
Pull you close where the shadows fall
Let the silence answer every call

[Verse 1]
An old guitar leans against the chair
With a sweet memory lingering in the air
I trace the map upon your weary hand
A quiet road that only we understand

[Pre-Chorus]
The hour is late and the world is slow
I just want to bask in your midnight glow
Every single look is drawing me near
Making all the edges disappear

[Chorus]
When I feel your breath on my neck
It is a warm rush I could never forget
We are wrapped in a slow and steady flame
And I just want to hear you whisper my name

[Post-Chorus]
